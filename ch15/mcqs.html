12.1 The potential is constant throughout a given region in space. Is the electric field zero or non-zero in this region? Explain.

For constant potential in a region, the electric field is zero, as,
  • V1 = V2
  • ΔV = V2 - V1 ⇒ ΔV = 0
  • Now, as V = Ed ⇒ E = V/d
  • E = 0
Hence, when potential difference is zero, electric field is also zero.
